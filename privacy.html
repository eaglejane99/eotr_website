Offline Smart Unit Converter

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how EAGLE ON THE ROCK APPS, a developer brand of EAGLE ON THE ROCK PTY LTD, handles information in relation to the Offline Smart Unit Converter mobile application.

2. Information the app uses

Offline Smart Unit Converter is designed to work without account registration or mandatory internet access. The app is intended to perform calculations and conversions directly on the device.

3. Personal data

The app does not require users to create an account or submit personal information to use the core conversion features. Favorites and recent conversion history are stored locally on the user’s device for convenience.

4. Local storage

To improve the user experience, the app may store settings such as selected theme, favorite unit pairs, and recent conversions locally on the device. This information is not intended to be sold or shared externally.

5. Third-party services

If future versions of the app include third-party services such as analytics, crash reporting, or advertising, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly. Users should review this page periodically for changes.

6. Children’s privacy

The app is a general utility tool and is not directed specifically to children under 13.

7. Security

Reasonable care is taken to keep the app simple and privacy-conscious. However, no software or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.
